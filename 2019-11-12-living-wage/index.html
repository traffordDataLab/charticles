Living wage

November 12, 2019

The percentage of employee jobs with hourly pay below the living wage in Trafford was 23.6% in 2018. This percentage is higher than England (22.9%) but similar to Greater Manchester (23.8%). Manchester recorded the lowest percentage of employee jobs with hourly pay below the living wage (18.1%) and Wigan the highest (31.5%).
