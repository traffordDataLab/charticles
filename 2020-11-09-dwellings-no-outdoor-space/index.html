Almost one in four Old Trafford households has no garden

November 09, 2020

Around 7% of households in Trafford have no access to a private or shared garden. This compares favourably with London where 21% of households have no garden. However, the proportion of dwellings without access to a garden varies considerably across Trafford. Almost 1 in 4 of Old Trafford households (22.6%) has no access to a private or shared garden. By contrast, nearly all households in Timperley North have a garden (99.4%).

The average size of private home gardens varies across Trafford too. In Old Trafford the median size private garden is 49.3 m2 whilst in Hale Barns it is 436.9 m2.
