CO2 emissions

November 5, 2019

In 2017, the industry and commercial sector accounted for 53.2% of CO2 emissions in Trafford. In Greater Manchester this sector contributed 31.2% and 35.2% in England. CO2 emissions since 2005 have fallen by 29% in Trafford, 45% in Greater Manchester and 47% in England.
