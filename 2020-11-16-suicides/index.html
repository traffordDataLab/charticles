Trafford's suicide rate tracks England

November 16, 2020

Over the three-year period between 2017 and 2019 Trafford registered 49 deaths by suicide. At 8.1 suicides per 100,000 population (95% CI 6.0, 10.7), this is the lowest age-standardised suicide rate amongst Greater Manchester's local authorities. However, whilst the trend in the suicide rate has remained significantly lower than the North West over the last few years it remains at a rate that isn't statistically different from England's.

If you are having a difficult time or you are worried about someone else you can call the Samaritans for free on 116 123, email them at jo@samaritans.org, or visit www.samaritans.org to find your nearest branch.

Age-standardised rates are used to allow comparison between populations which may contain different proportions of people of different ages.
