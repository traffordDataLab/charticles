Median house prices

October 18, 2018

The median house price in Trafford in 2018 was £263,500. At ward level this varies between £165,000 in Bucklow-St Martins and £650,000 in neighbouring Bowdon. The ratio of median house prices to median workplace-based gross annual earnings in Trafford in 2018 was 9.4. This suggests that full-time workers could expect to pay an estimated 9.4 times their annual workplace-based earnings to purchase a home in Trafford.
