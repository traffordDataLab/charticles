Air pollution levels during COVID-19

April 30, 2020

During the COVID-19 pandemic the UK started a lockdown on 23th of March 2020. Low levels of pollution have been reported attributed to reduced road traffic and industrial activity across the UK. During the lockdown, the weekly mean on levels of nitrogen dioxide (NO2) recorded by the monitoring station on the A56 in Trafford has remained lower than the weekly mean levels during the same period for the previous two years.
