Claimant Rate

July 13, 2020

The Claimant Count indicates the number of people claiming benefits principally for the reason of being unemployed. From March 2020 to May 2020 there was an additional 3.4% of the population aged 16 and over claiming benefits related to unemployment in the UK reaching 6.4% on May 2020. In Trafford the increase was of 2.9% reaching 5.4%.

The Trafford's wards that previous to the COVID-19 outbreak had high percentage of claimants where the ones experiencing the largest increments with Clifford reaching 10.3 % and Longford doubling their number of claims. However all the wards reported an increased number of claimants with half of them now having more that 5% of the over 16s population claiming unemployment related benefits.
