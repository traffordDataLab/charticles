Crime rate

October 28, 2019

There were 81 recorded crimes per 1,000 population (19,178 offences) in Trafford during the year ending June 2019. This is below the 117 crimes per 1,000 population recorded in Greater Manchester (328,268 offences). The rate of recorded crime in Trafford is lower than Greater Manchester across all categories of crime with the exception of bicycle theft which is slightly higher.
