Relative deprivation

November 1, 2019

5.1% of small areas (7 out of 138) in Trafford are amongst the 10% most deprived nationally on the 2019 Index of Multiple Deprivation. This represents a relative increase since the 2015 release when 2.9% (4 out of 138) of small areas were ranked in the bottom 10%.

Trafford 017E in Bucklow-St Martins ward was the most deprived small area in Trafford in 2019 ranking 490th out of 32,844 small areas in England. This is an increase in relative deprivation since 2015 when it was ranked 679th.
